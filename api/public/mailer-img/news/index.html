The Most Important Thing in a Company
 

A highly capable and hard-working employee will not be able to reach its highest potential in an environment that is degrading and demotivating.

One way I have avoided this is by appreciating performers at the end of the month. A token of gratitude in the form of incentives is given to them which makes them feel seen.

This simple practice is highly effective in boosting productivity and general happiness in the office.

Happy employees are productive employees./p>

Ask yourself whether your employer appreciates you or not, and if not, the next time you are job hunting keep this in mind.

I thank you for being patient and reading the newsletter.

Sachin Mittal

(CEO and Founder of Loanwalle)
